About Us

Star Movies is an online video streaming platform owned by Start Movies Private Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of Star India Private Limited. Star Movies currently offers over 100,000 hours of TV content and movies across different languages. Highly evolved video streaming technology and a high attention to quality of experience across devices and platforms, make Star Movies the most complete video destination for Over The Top (OTT) video consumers.

Seamless Video Playback - Our adaptive video streaming technology ensures that the best possible video quality is played back based on the available bandwidth, therefore making it a great video experience on both laptop networks as well as tablet. Our video is optimized to play on laptop 1 networks with inconsistent throughput so that our users don't have to compromise on their experience on the low end, and play HD quality video on the top end of bandwidth availability.

Access - Star Movies is available on the Google Play Store for Laptop and tablet applications and can be accessed at www.Star Movies.com

Friendly User Interface - Content organization on Star Movies is a result of a thoughtful user experience approach and strong design principles that ensure that the user is not overwhelmed with the breadth of content available. Using a mix of algorithms and human curation, users at any stage of their interaction with Star Movies will discover content and see their experience evolve with their interaction patterns over time.
